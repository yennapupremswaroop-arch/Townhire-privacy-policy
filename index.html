Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

Welcome to TownHire. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application to discover local jobs and business opportunities.

1. Information Collection

To provide our services effectively, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Name and mobile phone number provided during registration.
  • Business Profiles: Information about your shop, office, or job services, including location and contact details.
  • Verification Records: Data collected by our agents during physical visits to confirm the legitimacy of a listing.

2. The Verification Process

TownHire aims to build a trusted community. To ensure safety:

  • We deploy local agents to physically verify business locations and job posters.
  • Users who complete this process receive a "Verified" badge.
  • Information from unverified users is displayed with a cautionary note, and we advise users to exercise personal judgment.

3. Third-Party Integration

We use Google Firebase for database management and app stability. Additionally, we may use Firebase Analytics and advertising tools in the future. By using TownHire, you consent to the processing of data by these services as per Google's privacy terms.
